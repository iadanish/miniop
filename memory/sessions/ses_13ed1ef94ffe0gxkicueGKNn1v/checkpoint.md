## §7 Discovered knowledge (cross-task)
_Facts learned during this session that may apply to future tasks. Items here are candidates for promotion to projects/<pid>/MEMORY.md if they prove durable._

### Discovered
- **faster-whisper `task="translate"`** translates foreign speech to English; `task="transcribe"` transcribes as-is. For English input, use `transcribe`.
- **Microphone selection at startup** is important on Windows — multiple input devices can exist; let user choose.
- **`requests` is a transitive dependency** of faster-whisper (via huggingface_hub) but not always installed automatically — must include in requirements.txt.
- **Audio recording needs a while-loop** in the InputStream context manager; `pass` exits immediately with no frames captured.
